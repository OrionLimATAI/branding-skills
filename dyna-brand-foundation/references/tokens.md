# Dyna.Ai Brand Tokens

Use these tokens as the shared baseline for Dyna.Ai branded work. Follow the most specific official PDF or scene skill when it gives more precise channel guidance.

## Core Identity

- Brand name: `Dyna.Ai`
- Brand color: `#006CEE`
- Typeface: `Poppins`
- Logo clear space: at least `0.5b`, where `b` is logo height
- Logo minimum size: `10mm` high for print, `28px` high for mobile/online, `1in` high for TV/video

## Color Roles

- `#006CEE`: primary Dyna.Ai brand color
- `#000000`: high-contrast type, core dark surfaces, strict brand contrast
- `#FFFFFF`: primary clean background and type on dark/blue surfaces
- `#9ABCFF`: light blue support color
- Yellow: attention, threshold, anomaly, or warning only
- Red and green: conventional negative and positive status semantics only

## Typography

Use Poppins for brand-facing work. Do not introduce unrelated decorative, novelty, or generic product fonts unless a channel source explicitly requires it.

## Language Standard

Prefer specific over vague, proof over hype, capability over magic, clear role over implied endorsement, and business outcome over abstract transformation.

## Forbidden Patterns

- Do not rename the brand or write inconsistent capitalization.
- Do not treat `#006CEE` as temporary or channel-only.
- Do not distort, recolor, crop, stretch, or recreate logo artwork.
- Do not place important text over busy or low-contrast imagery.
- Do not invent metrics, customer proof, awards, claims, or partner endorsements.
- Do not use generic AI cliches when a specific Dyna.Ai business or fintech context is available.
